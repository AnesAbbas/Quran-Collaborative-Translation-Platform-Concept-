# Qur’an Crowd Translation Platform

## Overview

The **Qur’an Crowd Translation Platform** is a collaborative system for creating, refining, and studying translations of the Qur’an across many languages. The goal is to combine **community contribution**, **transparent discussion**, and **scholarly review** to improve accessibility and understanding while preserving trust and accuracy.

Unlike static translation websites, this platform allows users to **propose, compare, and refine translations** in a structured environment with clear review processes.

---

## Goals

* Expand Qur’an translation coverage to **more languages and dialects**
* Improve translation quality through **collaboration and iteration**
* Provide **transparent reasoning and discussion** behind translation choices
* Bridge **community participation and scholarly oversight**

---

## Key Features

### 1. Multi-Translation Comparison

Users can view multiple translations of a verse side-by-side to observe differences in wording and interpretation.

### 2. Community Translation Proposals

Contributors can submit alternative translations for verses or phrases in different languages.

### 3. Voting and Feedback

Community members can evaluate translations based on:

* clarity
* linguistic accuracy
* faithfulness to the Arabic text

### 4. Scholar Review

Qualified reviewers can mark translations as:

* **Verified**
* **Under Review**
* **Debated**

### 5. Word-Level Insights

Each Arabic word can link to:

* root and morphology
* possible meanings
* occurrences in other verses

### 6. Discussion Layer

Each verse includes a structured discussion area where contributors can explain translation choices and reference classical commentary.

---

## Trust & Governance

To maintain reliability, the platform uses a **tiered contribution model**:

1. Community suggestions
2. Community voting and discussion
3. Scholar or moderator review

Translations remain **transparent and traceable**, showing contributor history and review status.

---

## Target Users

* Translators and linguists
